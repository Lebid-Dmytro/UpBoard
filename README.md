The main page where you can add tasks, view the archive, check all available tasks by their status ("To do", "In progress", "Done"). You can also leave a review. 

<img width="522" alt="home_page" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/6436f70f-f901-4a44-95d4-eb80f8fffff6" />
<hr/>

You must be authorized to add or see the list of tasks.

<img width="1440" alt="authorization" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/41218b44-3a76-41a0-9742-cfd65f3aa2bb" />
<hr/>

When creating a task, you fill in all available fields. The employee who added the task and his company are added automatically.

<img width="1370" alt="create_task" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/bf4e5d94-227e-4ea0-9050-672ccee0be32" />
<hr/>

The archive page includes all tasks that were closed more than 30 days ago. That is, the tasks remain on the "Done" page for no more than 30 days.

<img width="1425" alt="archive" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/b10393fc-3896-4305-a24f-3c1d9e306dd1" />
<hr/>

After choosing one of the three statuses, you will be taken to a page with a list of all tasks with this status. In the list of all tasks, you can see data: name, description, image, date the task was added, the employee who added it, and the number of comments under this task.

<img width="1422" alt="tasks_list" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/8baee7e7-8d33-4b54-affc-bb464ac2271d" />
<hr/>

Next, you can view all the details of one task, change the status, and comment or reply to a comment.

<img width="1414" alt="task_detail" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/7c9effa6-c197-4299-9dad-938afbc5baf8" />
<img width="1440" alt="task_detail_and_comments" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/e3c2765b-8708-4599-9541-90bb3cb39abf" />
